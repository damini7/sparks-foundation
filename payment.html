Raising Hope

To help Ukraine , Millions more are displaced within the country, facing harsh winter conditions and attacks on civilian-populated areas. Eight years of armed conflict in eastern Ukraine had already created a humanitarian crisis.

Why Donate?

More than 7 million people have fled Ukraine in response to Russia's invasion, making this the largest refugee crisis in Europe.

All donations to the Ukraine Crisis Relief Fund will support humanitarian assistance in impacted communities in Ukraine and surrounding regions where Ukrainian refugees have fled.

How Your Donation Will Be Used ?
Your donation to the Ukraine Crisis Relief Fund will provide:
Shelter, food, and clean water for refugees
Health and psychosocial support
Access to education and economic assistance
And more
